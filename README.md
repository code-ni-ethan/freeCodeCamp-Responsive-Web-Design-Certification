
# Responsive Web Design Certification   

Hi, I am Ethan. This repository is meant to document my journey in the freeCodeCamp free Responsive Web Design Certification course.


## 🚀 About Me
I'm an 18 year old programmer who's just really starting out at things. I'm still learning a myriad of languages one step at a time to avoid burning myself out, and really it's been a fun journey! I've been challenging myself to take coding exercises head-on with minimal to no AI assistance. I believe it is the greatest violation I could do to fully rely on AI when my parents are literally financing me to take education in I.T.

God bless!
